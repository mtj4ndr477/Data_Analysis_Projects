Interpretation note (reframed for team):

  • ≈ 0 → Distribution is symmetrical → Either mean or median is appropriate.
  • > 0.5 → Moderately right-skewed → Median is more robust.
  • > 1 → Strongly right-skewed → Median is recommended.
  • < -0.5 → Left-skewed → Mean may be acceptable, but requires caution.

Based on the team’s findings, the CREDIT_LIMIT distribution is strongly right-skewed (skewness > 1). Therefore, we chose the median for imputation to minimize the impact of outliers.
